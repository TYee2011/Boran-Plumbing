# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
A balanced mix of residential homeowners and commercial property managers/operations directors. Homeowners require prompt, clean, and highly professional installations and repairs. Commercial clients need technical precision, pressure/code compliance, rapid emergency dispatch, and straightforward upfront digital billing/quoting.

## Product Purpose
To act as the primary marketing, credibility, and customer acquisition engine for Boran Plumbing. The site must convey elite craftsmanship and reliability, convert visitor interest into direct telephone calls or structured online service bookings, and validate service coverage dynamically.

## Brand Personality
*   **Absolute Reliability**: Trustworthy, licensed, and highly professional; no shortcuts.
*   **Technical Precision**: Highly competent, utilizing advanced diagnostic methods (like thermal and acoustic correlation leak detection) and executing pristine physical plumbing layouts.
*   **Restrained & Elite**: Clear, clean, and authoritative copy paired with high-quality visual representations of tradesmanship.

## Anti-references
*   Cluttered directory templates or lead-generation landing pages with aggressive spam-like overlays.
*   Older local service styles featuring cheap neon-colored badges, comic-sans typography, or flashing emergency tags.
*   Vague, text-only layouts filled with fake-looking illustrations or stock vectors that lack credibility.

## Design Principles
*   **Show the Craft**: Highlight the physical craftsmanship of plumbing (clean lines, copper joints, polished materials) as design elements rather than relying on generic stock elements.
*   **Utilitarian Clarity**: Information, emergency phone numbers, and booking affordances must be accessible and readable on any screen without cognitive friction.
*   **Pricing & Process Transparency**: Keep steps explicit and digital quote estimates straightforward to build instant consumer trust.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
*   Contrast levels must satisfy WCAG AA standards (minimum 4.5:1 for body and 3:1 for large display elements) to assist older homeowners.
*   Forms must have clear, high-contrast labels positioned above inputs and readable helper instructions.
*   Reduced motion preferences must be fully respected (`prefers-reduced-motion: reduce`) for users with sensitivity to animations.
